The Silent Crisis: Our Unorganized Waste Ecosystem

This section highlights the key challenges in the current waste management system that BinGo aims to solve. Explore the cards to understand the inefficiencies we are tackling.

Chaos & Inefficiency

Scrap disposal in India is largely unorganized, leading to significant environmental and economic inefficiencies.

Lack of Transparency

"Kabadiwalas" often operate offline, resulting in inconsistent pricing and no clear tracking for waste generators.

Untapped Potential

Industries struggle with managing recyclable waste, missing out on potential revenue and sustainability goals.

Recycler's Dilemma

Verified and clean scrap is hard to source efficiently, creating a bottleneck for the entire recycling industry.

Introducing BinGo: Where Waste Meets Innovation

List Your Scrap

Users, whether from households or industries, can easily list their recyclable waste by type (metal, plastic, e-waste, etc.) on our intuitive app and web platform. Just a few taps and your scrap is ready for collection.

Smart Pickup Scheduling

Request a pickup at your convenience. Our GPS-based system instantly connects you with the nearest verified collector ("kabadiwala") or recycler, ensuring a prompt and efficient service.

Dynamic Pricing

No more guesswork or unfair rates. Get real-time, fair prices based on current market rates, material type, and weight. Transparency is at the core of every transaction.

Track & Transact

Monitor your pickup in real-time from the moment it's assigned. Once completed, receive an instant digital receipt and access a complete history of all your transactions.
